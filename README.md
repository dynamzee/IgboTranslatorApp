🇳🇬 Dyna Igbo Translator: Putting My Language on the Map
Dyna Igbo Translator is more than just a piece of code; it is a labor of love and a technical bridge built to elevate the Igbo language in the digital age. This project embodies my journey as a developer and my commitment to ensuring that my heritage has a prominent place in the world of technology.

🌟 Why This Project Stands Out
Putting a language on the map requires more than just a dictionary—it requires a "sweet" User Experience (UX) that understands the nuances of human speech.

Cultural Identity: This app is an embodiment of my personal mission to preserve and promote the Igbo language.

Intelligent Matching: Unlike static search bars, this app uses advanced logic to find the best translation even when the user makes a typo.

Community-Powered: It features a built-in suggestion system, because I believe the best way to grow a language is through the collective wisdom of its speakers.

🛠️ The "Gold Standard" Tech Stack
To ensure a seamless experience, I leveraged an extensive suite of Python's most powerful libraries:

Module	Purpose in the App
spacy	Used for Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand the structure of English sentences before translation.
difflib	Powers the "Close Match" logic, ensuring that even if you misspell a word, the app finds the right Igbo equivalent.
unicodedata	Essential for handling Igbo diacritics and special characters, ensuring every accent mark is technically perfect.
re (Regex)	Used for advanced text cleaning and pattern matching to strip out noise and focus on the meaning.
Flask	The backbone of the web application, connecting the Python logic to a crisp, modern UI.
